TITLE: Academic Support Services (Overview)
TAMU-CC offers a variety of instructional methods and educational services to help retain and graduate Islanders. See: https://www.tamucc.edu/academics/support/index.php


Tutoring & Learning Services (CASA) — Director: Dr. Noelle Ballmer, (361) 825-2254, noelle.balmer@tamucc.edu


Writing Center (WC) — Assistance with any aspect of writing across disciplines


Structured Learning Assistance (SLA) — Required supplemental sessions for selected gateway courses; contact Dr. Noelle Ballmer


Supplemental Instruction (SI) — Peer-assisted study sessions for traditionally difficult subjects; request a semester in advance


Tutoring — CASA supports high-challenge and prerequisite courses


Academic Coaching & Mentoring — One-on-one support focusing on learning strategies and barriers


First-Year Learning Community Mentors — Embedded academic and social transition support


First Islander Scholars’ Academy (FISA) — Support for first-generation students; contact Dr. Noelle Ballmer


Island Harbor — Supervised Independent Living (SIL) in partnership with DFPS for foster-care alumni


TRiO SSS Teacher Prep — Director: Marisa Scanlon, (361) 825-4571, marisa.scanlon@tamucc.edu


TRiO SSS STEM — Director: Heather Herrera, (361) 825-4332, heather.herrera@tamucc.edu


Viking Islander Program (VIP) — Co-enrollment with Del Mar College guaranteeing full admission upon requirements completion
